第壱話

THE RULES OF CHAOS

In the neon-soaked streets of 2099 Japan, an illegal racing competition known as BAMPAKA has erupted from the underground. Four districts. Four drivers. No rules. The streets themselves become weapons — fractured highways through Nagano's frozen mountains, Osaka's chaotic urban sprawl, Tokyo's digital mazes, and Nara's ancient death corridors.

「勝者のみが生き残る」

第弐話

THE PRIZE

The winner receives 天皇の鍵 — The Emperor's Key — a digital artifact that grants access to Japan's central traffic control network. With it, you don't just win races. You own the roads.

01

THE DANGER

Every race is a war zone. Vehicles are weaponized. Districts are hostile. One wrong turn and you're scrap metal on the Rainbow Bridge.

02

THECODE

Every driver carries a unique racing signal — a digital graffiti tag broadcast on every screen they pass. Your code is your identity. Your identity is your weapon.

THE WAR

The corporations want BAMPAKA shut down. The people want it freed. The drivers? They just want to race — and they'll burn the whole country down to do it.
